About Me

Hey there! My name is Matthew Maung, and I am a Junior at NYU studying Computer Science.

Throughout my career as a college student, I have had the opportunity to culminate a multifaceted set of skills and experiences through my extracurriculars and by studying abroad. Throughout 2023, I have had the opportunity to study abroad in both South Korea at Korea University and China and NYU Shanghai. I navigated through language barriers and cultural differences, learning how to navigate foreign cities and adapt. I also participated in a research internship at Korea University, furthering my understanding of topics such as machine learning and large language models.

In addition I have had the opportunity to explore multiple disciplines outside of my major. I was the secretary of NYU’s Asian Cultural Union, learning the importance of marketing to small businesses in order to obtain partnerships. I also was the Airframe subteam lead for Rogue Aerospace, learning about what it means to be a project manager while also understanding how to be a detail oriented person. Lastly, I was also a Social Media Manager, developing valuable skills in UX/UI Design, and learning what it takes to create captivating content.

Over the summer, I had the opportunity to work at Novartis as a facilities management intern, where I applied data analytics to solve different problems that existed on its campus. I polished my skills in excel, forecasting future expenses and pitching new ideas to the Directors of the East Hanover campus, but also learning how to utilize storytelling as a way to deliver exciting pitches to audiences.

I am someone who is willing to learn anything no matter how difficult or unfamiliar it may be. I apply my experiences and skills that I have gained throughout my college career to innovate within the spaces I enter. I am a driven individual, open to take on new challenges, and would love to learn more about opportunities at your company that you think best fit me. I am open to positions related to Data Science/Analysis, Consulting, and Software Engineering.
